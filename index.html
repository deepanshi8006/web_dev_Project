Our History

Founded by Abcd, our coffee shop was born out of a passion for high-quality, ethically sourced coffee and a commitment to community. What started as a small local venture has grown into a beloved spot where quality and comfort meet. Our beans are carefully selected from top coffee-growing regions around the world, ensuring that each cup is a journey through rich, aromatic flavors. From the bold intensity of our espresso to the smooth richness of our cold brew, every sip is a testament to our dedication to perfection.

As we continue to grow and evolve, we remain focused on our core values: delivering exceptional coffee, fostering community connections, and creating a space where everyone feels at home. We are excited about the future and look forward to many more years of serving our customers and contributing to our community. Thank you for being a part of our journey. We invite you to visit us at New York and experience the passion and dedication that have been the cornerstone of our success.
